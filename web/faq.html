Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about shopping with FashionAI Shop.

We offer two convenient shipping options for all orders:

Standard Delivery (2-5 business days) — This is our most popular option. Orders are processed within 24 hours and delivered to your door in 2-5 business days. Standard delivery is FREE on orders over 1,000 UAH. For orders below this threshold, a flat shipping fee of 79 UAH applies.

Express Delivery (1-2 business days) — Need your order urgently? Our express service guarantees delivery within 1-2 business days. Express shipping costs 150 UAH regardless of order size.

All orders come with full tracking so you can monitor your delivery in real time.

We make returns simple and stress-free. Our 30-day return policy means you have a full month from the date of delivery to return any item you're not completely satisfied with.

To initiate a return:
1. Contact our support team at support@fashionai.shop with your order number.
2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging (if possible).
3. Ensure the item is unworn, unwashed, and has all original tags attached.
4. Drop off the parcel at your nearest courier service.

Refund timeline: Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days back to your original payment method.

Note: Sale items and customized products are non-refundable unless they are defective.

FashionAI Shop accepts a wide variety of payment methods for your convenience:

Credit & Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, and American Express are all accepted. Your card details are encrypted using SSL technology for maximum security.

PayPal: Pay securely using your PayPal account without sharing your financial information directly.

Telegram Payments: Shop directly through our Telegram bot and pay using integrated Telegram Payments — quick, secure, and convenient.

Bank Transfer: For larger orders, we also accept direct bank transfers. Contact support for banking details.

All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your full card details on our servers.

Delivery times depend on your chosen shipping method and location:

Standard Delivery: 2-5 business days (most orders within Ukraine arrive in 2-3 days).
Express Delivery: 1-2 business days.
Remote Areas: Standard delivery to remote regions may take up to 7 business days.

Order Processing: All orders placed before 2:00 PM on business days are processed and dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends are processed the next business day.

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and/or Telegram notification to monitor your delivery status in real time.

Currently, FashionAI Shop primarily ships within Ukraine. However, we are actively expanding our international shipping capabilities.

Currently available international destinations:
• Poland
• Germany
• France
• United Kingdom (post-Brexit customs may apply)

International shipping rates and times vary by destination. Typically, international orders take 7-14 business days. Customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's import regulations — these are the buyer's responsibility.

For international orders or if your country is not listed, please contact us at support@fashionai.shop and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Tracking your order is easy! Here's how:

Via Email: Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with your tracking number and a direct link to the carrier's tracking page.

Via Telegram Bot: If you purchased through our Telegram bot, you can use the /orders command to see your order status in real time.

Via Website: Log in to your account on our website and navigate to "My Orders" to view current order status and tracking information.

Via Support: You can also contact our support team at support@fashionai.shop with your order number and we'll provide an immediate update.

Tracking information is typically available within 24 hours of dispatch.

We want to make sure your clothes fit perfectly! Our sizes follow European/Ukrainian standard sizing.

Women's T-Shirts & Tops:
XS = EU 34 | S = EU 36 | M = EU 38 | L = EU 40 | XL = EU 42 | XXL = EU 44

Men's Shirts & T-Shirts:
XS = chest 86cm | S = 91cm | M = 96-101cm | L = 106-111cm | XL = 116cm | XXL = 122cm

Jeans & Pants: Listed by waist/inseam measurements in cm.

Dresses: Follow women's top sizing.

Shoes: Listed in EU sizes. See the specific product page for shoe width options.

Not sure? Our AI chatbot (bottom-right corner) can help you find the right size based on your measurements!

Yes, you can cancel your order — but the cancellation window is time-sensitive:

Before Processing (within 1 hour of placing the order): You can cancel freely with a full refund. Contact us immediately via support@fashionai.shop or through our Telegram bot.

After Processing but Before Dispatch: Cancellation may still be possible if the order hasn't been handed to the courier yet. Contact support urgently.
